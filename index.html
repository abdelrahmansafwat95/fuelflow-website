How it works

Three steps from pump to dashboard.

No cards to issue. No PINs to leak. No end-of-month reconciliation marathons.

1

Driver scans

Driver pulls up to the pump and scans its QR code in the FuelFlow Driver App — or taps an NFC card.

2

System authorizes

Budget check + 6 fraud rules in parallel, in milliseconds. Approved fuel-ups unlock the pump.

3

Fleet sees it live

The transaction lands in the dashboard before the driver walks away. Receipts, in real time.
