How it works

Three taps from pulling up to driving off.

1 · Scan QR

Open the app and point at the pump QR code. Done.

2 · Approved

Budget check, fraud rules — all in under 200ms.

3 · Drive

Receipt lands in the dashboard before you pull away.

Built around how drivers actually work

The little things that matter.

Bilingual

Toggle English / Arabic any time. Full RTL — every screen flips, not just the words.

Permanent QR card

Get a printable, laminate-able QR card for the dashboard. Useful when your phone is dead or out of signal.

Offline-tolerant

Lost signal? The app retries the authorization automatically when you're back online — no lost transactions.

No payment cards on device

Identity is bound to the vehicle, not to a card. If your phone is lost, nothing leaks.
