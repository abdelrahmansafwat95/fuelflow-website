03 · FRAUD ENGINE

Fraud detection that actually fires

Six rules run in parallel on every authorization request, each tuned to a real abuse pattern we've seen on Egyptian forecourts.

  • Velocity — too many fills in too little time
  • Geo-jump — impossible travel between stations
  • Duplicate — same pump, same vehicle, twice
  • Excessive litres — fills above tank capacity
  • Hourly cap — odd-hour pattern detection
  • New vehicle — first-use scrutiny on fresh vehicles

04 · DRIVER QR

Printable QR codes per driver

No physical cards to issue, lose, or share. Each driver gets a unique QR — printable, laminate-able, rotate-able. No PII on the print. Tied to the vehicle in the system, not the driver's wallet.

  • Rotate codes per driver, per vehicle, or per route
  • Revoke a compromised code in one click
  • Works alongside NFC card support
