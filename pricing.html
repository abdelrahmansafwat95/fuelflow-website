Common questions

Things people ask before buying.

How is pricing calculated?

Per active vehicle, billed monthly in EGP. Volume discounts apply at 100+ vehicles and again at 500+. No setup fees, no minimums beyond your plan.

Are stations charged?

No. The platform is free for stations during pilot, and revenue-share or per-transaction afterwards depending on partnership terms. Stations get more corporate volume — we share in that, not in a fixed subscription.

What's the contract length?

Monthly by default. Annual contracts come with a discount. We don't lock you into multi-year commitments — if FuelFlow isn't working, we'd rather know.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee for Pilot or Scale. Enterprise onboarding (on-site training, custom integrations) is quoted separately.
