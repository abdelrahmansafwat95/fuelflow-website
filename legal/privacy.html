Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FuelFlow ("FuelFlow", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our websites, web applications, mobile apps, and APIs (collectively, the "Services").

1. Who is the data controller

FuelFlow, headquartered in Cairo, Egypt, is the data controller. For privacy questions, write to info@foxystems.com.

2. What we collect

  • Account data — name, phone number (+20 format), email, role, company affiliation.
  • Operational data — vehicles, drivers, stations, attendants, fuel transactions you create or that your organization assigns to you.
  • Technical data — device type, app version, IP address, approximate location (only at the moment of authorization).
  • Communications — messages you send via our forms, email, or WhatsApp.

3. How we use it

  • To operate the platform — authorize transactions, run fraud checks, generate receipts and reports.
  • To authenticate users via SMS OTP.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To comply with Egyptian law, including Central Bank of Egypt guidelines and tax requirements.
  • To improve product quality through aggregated, de-identified analytics.

4. What we never do

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.
  • We do not share driver location data outside the authorization purpose.
  • We do not use your operational data to train models without an explicit, separately negotiated agreement.

5. Sharing

We share data only with: (a) your own organization's authorized users; (b) stations participating in a transaction you initiate; (c) processors acting on our behalf under written agreements (SMS gateways, cloud infrastructure, observability tools); (d) authorities when required by Egyptian law.

6. Data residency & retention

Operational data is stored in Egypt. Transaction records are retained for the period required by tax and CBE-aligned regulations. Audit logs are retained for the same period. You can request deletion of account data subject to those obligations.

7. Security

See our security page for the technical details. In short: TLS in transit, AES-256-GCM for PII at rest, bcrypt for passwords, RBAC, full audit log.

8. Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your personal data by emailing info@foxystems.com. We respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies & analytics

We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible / PostHog without third-party cookies). A cookie banner appears only when consent is legally required.

10. Changes

We'll post material changes to this policy here and notify account admins by email before they take effect.
